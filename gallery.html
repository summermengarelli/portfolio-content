A key piece of the Pomodoro method is to take a break every 25 minutes...and you really should take those breaks! It is recommended that you stop when your timer goes off, even if you are in a flow state in your work, to prevent burnout. This can mean doing whatever is most relaxing or enjoyable for you, or whatever will allow you to recharge before your next pomodoro â€” eating a snack, talking to a friend, or even just scrolling TikTok.
